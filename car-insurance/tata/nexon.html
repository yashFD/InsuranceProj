Tata Nexon Car Insurance

Third party insurance is the most basic insurance policy that is mandatory to be owned in the country, as per the Indian Motor Tariff. This cover provides financial assistance to you for the expenses, which you are liable to pay to a third party for any damage or loss caused due to an accident, involving your car. Driving a car without third party insurance can get you penalised with a fine of Rs. 2,000 and/or imprisonment of up to 3 months.

Types of Insurance Cover

1. Third Party Cover

This is the minimum insurance cover required to be owned by everyone in India, according to the Indian Motor Tariff. It offers compensation for your financial expenses, which may incur by you towards the third party for bodily injury or death along with property damage, arising due to an accident with your car. The maximum amount of coverage for the expenses of property damage can go up to Rs. 7.5 lakh.

2. Standalone Own-Damage Cover

This is an insurance cover, which is designed to provide you with financial security for damage or loss sustained by your insured car. It gives you coverage for expenses which may arise due to damage or loss incurred by your car in an accident or a mishap.

3. Comprehensive Cover

This is a complete insurance package which offers coverage for all your expenses including the one incurred towards the third party as well as for damages sustained by your own car due to an accident or any unforeseen incident.

    How to register a claim for my Tata Nexon car insurance plan?

    To register the claim, you can follow the below mentioned easy steps:

      Step 1: If an unfortunate incident like accident, theft or mishap happens, then ensure that you immediately register an FIR at a nearby police station as a copy would be required of it during the claim settlement procedure.

      Step 2: Intimate your insurance company about the accident. The documents that are required to be submitted at the time of filing for claim vary depending upon the unfortunate incident and the insurance company.

      Step 3: You need to then submit a duly filled claim form along with all the necessary documents to your insurance provider.

      Step 4: Afterwards, the insurance company would appoint a surveyor so as to gauge the amount of damage sustained by your car in the accident or mishap. Then, a report would be submitted by the appointed surveyor based upon the analysis to the insurance company.

      Step 5: Once your insurer goes through the report, they would approve the claim in case everything goes fine. After approval, you are allowed to take your damaged car to any of the network garages to get it repaired.

    The coverages highlighted below are covered under the Tata Nexon Car insurance plan:

    • Loss or damage sustained by the insured vehicle due to any kind of man-made disaster like riots, vandalism etc

    • Own damage cover
