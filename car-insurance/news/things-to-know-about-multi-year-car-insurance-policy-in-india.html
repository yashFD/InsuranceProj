Things to Know About Multi Year Car Insurance Policy in India

Published On Jan 09, 2020 5:30 AM By InsuranceDekho

There are several benefits of a multi year car insurance policy. This guide will help you to know why you should buy a long term third party motor insurance policy.

Multi-Year Car Insurance Policy In India - As per the Indian Motor Tariff 2002, it is mandatory for new car owners to get a 3-year third party car insurance policy for being able to drive on the Indian streets. Apart from helping you fulfill the legal requirement, multi-year car insurance offers you convenience in the sense that you need not go through all the hassles of documentation and verification each year. Another benefit of a long-term policy is that it enables you to save your money by featuring good discounts.

This means that whenever you are buying a new vehicle, you are required to buy a long-term policy having third party coverage. While for a new car, the minimum term period is 3 years, for a new two-wheeler, it is a minimum of 5 years. However, this mandatory rule is not declared for a comprehensive car insurance policy. People seeking a comprehensive OD (own damage) car insurance policy for getting financial coverage against damages caused to themselves and their vehicle, can either go for the yearly plan of the same or buy a multi-year plan.

Why Should I Buy a Multi-Year Third Party Car Insurance Policy?

As mentioned earlier, buying a multi-year third party policy is mandatory while buying a new car. Though you can buy such a policy from the car dealership itself, it is always recommended to compare different motor insurance plans online to get the best deal.

When it comes to old cars, owners often tend to buy yearly policies. However, it could be a better idea to opt for a multi-year policy for old cars as well. Though you might have to shell out a higher premium amount at the moment, it could reward you with multiple benefits in a long term. Below are a few benefits of buying multi-year insurance plans.

Benefits of a Multi-Year Car Insurance Policy

Having a multi-year third party insurance policy comes with a number of benefits, the primary being providing you with a financial backup for a longer period of time.

1. No need to rush for renewing policy every year

It is true that the one-time premium charge of your multi-year car insurance will be higher in comparison to buying or renewing it every year. However, you are relieved from renewing it yearly. If you pay for your insurance policy collectively in advance, you would be having the freedom to continue enjoying the benefits of the insurance policy of your vehicle for many years together.

2. Escape the price hike

The Indian government raises the premium amount of your third party car insurance policy every financial year. This makes the premium amount of a particular year greater than the previous year’s amount, thus making the policyholder payout greater sums than he or she did last year. This problem is dealt with if you have a multi-year car insurance policy. The savings made from a multi-year policy due to the one-time payment of the premium can range between 20-40%.

3. Discount on multi-year policies

While buying a multi-year motor insurance policy, you might get a discount since you are paying three years’ premium at once, chances are that the company would reward you with a significant discount. The NCB offered with a multi-year policy becomes 40% at the end of the third consecutive year rather than the 35% that applies to the annual policies.

4. Convenience of purchasing insurance online

Most people avoid doing their insurance work as the amount of paperwork and the procedures can be tiresome. However, with the option of purchasing your insurance policies online, you can do all this work at your convenience in your own home with little to no paperwork. Additionally, along with all the savings made with purchasing a multi-year policy, you make more savings by buying online as they have the best rates. This is because it allows insurance providers to cut out the cost of middlemen.

Policyholders must, however, keep in mind that they cannot cancel the subscription for a third party cover during its term period, except in cases of double insurance or in case you no longer use your car, either because you have sold it or because you have transferred the ownership to someone else.

Types of Multi-Year Car Insurance Policy

As per the Supreme Court verdict, you can take any of the below-mentioned three options for getting a car insurance policy for the long-term:

  • Option 1- Long-Term Third Party Only Cover: You can buy only a third party insurance policy for three years which is compulsory without an OD coverage.
  • Option 2- Long-Term Comprehensive Cover: You can purchase a comprehensive plan for a period of 3 years, along with a compulsory third party cover for the same period of time.
  • Option 3- 1 Year Comprehensive Cover: You can purchase a comprehensive car insurance policy for 1 year along with a third party liability policy for 3 years. This is often the preferred choice for most car owners.
