Car Insurance Quotes Online

Car Insurance Quotes - The cost of a car insurance quote is called the premium. The premium for a third party plan is fixed by the Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority (IRDAI), which varies on different engine capacities. However, the premium for standalone own-damage and comprehensive car insurance plans are based on a range of factors such as the extent of coverage, make, model and variant of the car, type of fuel of the car, RTO location, and so on.

What are the Factors Affecting Vehicle/Car Insurance Quotes?

There are a number of factors that affect the premium quote of car insurance. Some of them are as follow:

1. Type of the Plan

The premium of the plan depends on the coverage opted for. Wider the coverage, the greater the premium. There are three basic types of car insurance based on the coverage they provide:

  • Third Party Insurance: This type of insurance is compulsory and covers the cost of third party liabilities including bodily injuries, death and property damage.
  • Standalone Own-Damage Insurance: This covers the cost of the policyholders vehicle damage in case of a mishap but is not compulsory.
  • Comprehensive Car Insurance: This type of cover provides compensation for both third party liabilities and own-damage costs under a single premium making it the most hassle-free option of the three.

2. MMV of Your Car/Vehicle

The primary factor which affects the premium quote of your car insurance policy is your cars MMV - make, model and variant.

3. Fuel Type

Another factor that affects the car insurance premium quote of an insurance policy is the fuel type of the car/vehicle. Different premiums are charged for diesel cars and different premiums are quoted for petrol cars.

4. Policyholders Profession

Insurance companies give weightage to the correlation between the policyholders risk of accident and their professions and hence adjust the premium accordingly. People involved with professions such as cab drivers, delivery agents are usually at a higher side of risk and thus a relatively higher premium charged to me.

5. Policyholders Credit Score

Many insurance companies take into consideration the credit score of the applicant to determine the Car Insurance Premium.

6. Car Size

Generally larger cars are considered safer than smaller cars in accidents. Therefore, often insurance companies offer relatively lower premiums to bigger cars than smaller ones.

7. Cars Safety Rating

Buying a car with a high safety rating means can help you get a car insurance plan at a relatively lower premium due to increased safety and lower chances of meeting an accident.

8. Policyholders Driving History

If the owner of the car/vehicle has been in accidents or has made previous car insurance claims, the insurance company will tend to charge a relatively higher premium to such individuals.

FAQs About Car Insurance Quotes Online

  • What is the meaning of a car insurance quote?

    A car insurance quote is the premium estimate for the insurance plan opted by you and the information submitted.

  • Is the premium for comprehensive car insurance same for all type of cars?

    No, when it comes to premium for comprehensive car insurance, it is different for all cars. The premium cost for the plan varies depending upon the MMV (make, model and variant) of the car, the engine capacity of the car, age of the car, the add-ons opted for, and a number of other factors.

  • How can I reduce my car insurance premium amount?

    You can reduce your car insurance premium amount by following the below mentioned simple tips:

    • Avoid making small claims in order to keep your NCB intact.
    • Install anti-theft devices approved by ARAI (Automotive Research Association of India) in your car to get the discount.
    • Increase the amount of deductible to reduce the car insurance premium.
    • Ensure to transfer your No Claim Bonus during the renewal of your car insurance plan.

  • How the age of the car impact the car insurance premium cost?

    The Insured Declared Value (IDV) provided by a car insurance company for an old or aged car is lower than that of a new car. The car's age impacts the IDV due to the depreciation loss that has been endured by the car. Thus, the premium amount of aged cars is lower than that of new cars.

  • How to compare car insurance policy quotes at InsuranceDekho?

    At the website of InsuranceDekho, you can simply compare various car insurance policy quotes. All the car insurance policy quotes that appear to you are personalised as per the policy details shared. Thus, you can easily compare the car insurance plan quotes on the basis of the available insurance coverage and other valid features offered by the car insurance covers.

  • Is an online car insurance plan a valid document?

    Yes, as per the motor laws of India, a car insurance plan document that is issued online is legally valid. However, while purchasing the insurance plan, ensure that it has been purchased through an Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (IRDAI) registered insurance company.

  • What is the meaning of IDV in a car insurance plan?

    IDV or Insured Declared Value is the maximum sum that needs to be compensated by the insurance company during the time of claim in case the car gets damaged beyond repair or gets stolen.

  • Is the comparison of car insurance quotes free of cost?

    At InsuranceDekho, you get the facility of comparing different car insurance quotes offered by different insurance companies absolutely free of cost. However, while purchasing a car insurance plan, you are required to pay the premium amount.

  • Should I purchase car insurance having the lowest premium quote?

    No, the car insurance plan with the lowest premium quote may not be the best plan for you. An optimum car insurance plan is the one that fulfills your requirements concerning affordability, coverage, etc. Therefore, the premium quote is just another basis and not the only parameter for deciding the best car insurance plan.

  • Is the lowest premium quote the best car insurance quote?

    No, the lowest premium quote need not be the best car insurance quote. The best car insurance plan is the one that meets your requirements like coverage, affordability, etc in the best manner. Thus, the premium quote is one of the basis and not the sole parameter for determining the best car insurance plan.
