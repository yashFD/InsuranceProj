Renewal of Royal Sundaram Health Insurance

When you buy a health insurance policy, you gain different coverage benefits. These coverage benefits are available only when you continue renewing your policy on a regular basis. Therefore, it is important to renew your health insurance policy on time so that you do not lose out on your coverage.

To make sure policyholders do not face any issue while renewing their health insurance policy, Royal Sundaram has kept its entire renewal process easy and time-saving. Furthermore, the health insurance company also provides online renewal and offline renewal option to its policyholders.

Royal Sundaram Health Insurance Policy Online Renewal Process

To renew your Royal Sundaram health insurance policy online, follow the below-mentioned steps:

Step 1: Visit The Website

Visit Royal Sundaram General Insurance’s official website.

Step 2: Choose Renewal Option

Choose the ‘Insta Renew’ tab.

Step 3: Enter Required Details

Choosing ‘Insta Renew’ tab will take you to the Renew Policy Online page. Here, you will be required to enter your Policy Number and Expiry Date.

Step 4: View Policy Details And Premium

Take a look at your policy details and premium amount to confirm whether they are correct or not.

Step 5: Make Payment

Proceed with the payment of your health insurance policy renewal premium.

Step 6: Receive Confirmation

Your policy will be renewed on successful premium payment. Also, you will receive an email regarding the successful renewal.

Royal Sundaram Health Insurance Policy Renewal - Offline Process

Online renewal of health insurance is easy at Royal Sundaram. However, the health insurance company, keeping the diverse needs of policyholders in mind, provides the option of offline renewal as well.

If the offline renewal of your policy suits you well, go ahead and call on the toll-free number of Royal Sundaram, i.e 1860-425-0000 and inform all about your requirements.

Paying a quick visit to the nearest branch of Royal Sundaram is also a renewal option you can go with.

Benefits of Renewing Health Insurance With Royal Sundaram

There are multiple benefits of renewing health insurance at Royal Sundaram. The key advantages include:

  • Lifelong renewal of the policy
  • Hassle-free claim settlement
  • Free health checkup benefit
  • Vast coverage against medical expenses
  • Tax benefits based on Section 80D of the Income Tax Act
