Max Bupa Health Insurance Mediclaim Policy Premium Calculator

Medical costs have become quite prohibitive and an average middle-class man finds it difficult to bear the expenses which are incurred if there is a medical emergency. That is why a health insurance plan is bought. The plan covers the medical costs incurred in case you avail medical assistance. You have to choose the coverage that you want for the plan and pay the required premium. When it comes to the premium, it should be affordable so that the coverage can be availed. That is why calculating the premium of the plan beforehand is recommended.

How to Calculate the Premium of Max Bupa Policies Online?

Calculating the premium of a health insurance plan can be done through online premium calculators. You have to furnish some simple details and the premium would be calculated. Some of the details include the following –

  • Your age
  • Policy term
  • Medical history
  • Coverage details
  • Sum insured you need
  • The type of policy to buy

Once you enter these details, the premium would be calculated easily. The online premium calculator also allows to change the variables like coverage benefits, the sum insured, etc. to adjust the calculated premium

Why Calculate Health Insurance Premium Online?

Calculating the premiums payable for the health insurance plan is necessary because of the following reasons:

  • You can add or reduce coverage benefits and riders
  • You’ll come to know the exact expense on the policy before
  • You judge whether the premium would be affordable to pay or not
  • You can compare the different rates and choose the best insurance product
  • You can choose the best coverage level, which would be suitable for your pockets
  • You can make sure there would be no financial strain in continuing the health insurance policy
  • You can choose the most suitable coverage benefits at the most affordable premium

  • How much premium do I have to pay if I buy an individual health insurance policy from Max Bupa?

    Annual premiums for individual health insurance plans for Max Bupa start at Rs. 6,806 (for a person aged 25 years).

    Age, number of people, pre-existing diseases, and family history are the prime factors that affect the health insurance premium. Before going ahead with the policy purchase, learn that the premium increases with age. Additionally, that premium for individual coverage is less as compared to that of the family floater.

    Performing premium comparison to make an ideal health insurance choice would require you do the premium calculation in the first place and then compare policy coverage along with the exclusions.

    Premiums for Max Bupa Health Insurance plans depend on several factors including the age of the insured, policy term, the sum insured chosen, add-on cover(s), number of members covered under the policy, etc. 

  • Do I have to pay GST on premiums paid for Max Bupa Health Insurance plans?

    Yes. You are required to pay GST at the rate of 18% on purchasing Max Bupa Health Insurance plans.
