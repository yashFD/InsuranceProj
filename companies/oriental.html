Oriental Insurance Company Limited Overview

The Oriental Insurance Company Limited was established in 1947 in Mumbai. The organisation was completely owned by the Oriental Government Security Life Assurance Company Limited. The firm was formed to continue with the general insurance business. The general insurance company became a subsidiary from 1956 to 1973 of Life Insurance Corporation of India, till the time general insurance business got nationalised in India. During the year 2003, all the shares of Oriental insurance company which were held by the general insurance corporation of India were all transferred to the Central Government.

The Oriental Insurance Company Limited tends to operate its business smoothly and in an organised manner. The experienced and trained workforce of the firm is its strength which follows several disciplines and expertise. The company is highly specialized in offering insurance covers for huge projects such as power plants, chemical plants, steel and petrochemical. The general insurance organisation has built several types of insurance policies to cater to the requirements of rural as well as the urban population of the country. The insurance company boasts to render excellent customer support due to being equipped with a qualified and experienced staff.

Oriental Insurance Company Product Portfolio

Oriental Motor Insurance Policy

The motor insurance plans offered by the Oriental Insurance Company are divided under different segments which are private car insurance policy and two-wheeler insurance policy. The insurance plan offers coverage against the accidental loss or damage, man-made calamities and natural disasters incurred by the insured bike or car. The motor insurance policy provided by the company also covers the liability towards the third parties as well as the personal accident cover to the owner-driver. In addition to these, different types of add-on covers are also available to enhance the coverage of motor insurance plans, which can be availed by paying a little extra amount of premium.

Oriental Health Insurance Policy

Oriental Health Insurance Plans are designed to cover the medical expenses that may arise during the time of emergency. Different health insurance plans offer coverage for different persons such as individuals, family, senior citizens, children, parents-in-laws and so on. The coverage is given for in-patient hospitalisation, pre hospitalisation and post hospitalisation, daycare procedures, domiciliary hospitalisation, ambulance charges and so on. The insurance plans provided by the general insurance company are Happy Family Floater Policy, Oriental Mediclaim Policy, Oriental Super Health Top-Up Policy, Oriental Happy Cash Policy, Group Mediclaim Policy and so on. The company makes all its health insurance covers available at affordable prices.

Oriental Shopkeeper Insurance Policy

The Shopkeeper Insurance Policy provided by the Oriental Insurance Company gives coverage to all the possible risks and perils which are faced by different types of small as well as medium sized business houses and shop owners. The insurance plans are meant to provide overall protection to the property and the assets and belongings of the insured person in the business venture. The coverage offered with this insurance plan is for damages and losses sustained by the shops or business buildings due to fire, breakdown of electrical equipment, business interruption, personal accident, accidental external means and so on.

Oriental House Holder Insurance Policy

Home is not just a place but an emotion for everyone. This makes the Oriental House Holder Insurance Policy quite special as it is designed to ensure comprehensive protection against loss and accidental damages suffered by the building as well as its contents caused by multiple risks such as fire, house breaking, loss of baggage, personal accident, public liability, damages to personal Computer and laptop, and so on. The house holder insurance plan can be bought and renewed online with the digital facility by registering on the Oriental Insurance Portal and making the payment online through debit card, credit card or net banking.

Oriental Rural Insurance Policy

The Oriental Insurance Policy offers the rural insurance plan with Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY). This is an insurance plan which owns an objective to support sustainable production in the agriculture industry by providing financial assistance to farmers who are badly suffering due to the damage or loss of crops caused by unforeseen events. The policy also aims to stabilise the farmer’s income by helping them adopt modern and innovative agricultural tools and practices. This rural insurance policy is mandatory to own by loanee farmers who have obtained crop loans for notified crops and voluntary for non-loanee farmers that hold secured interest in the insured crop.
