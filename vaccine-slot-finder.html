How Can I Get COVID-19 Vaccination Using CoWIN?

Follow the below-mentioned steps to get vaccinated for COVID-19 using CoWIN:

Step 1: Login and Register
  • Login to www.cowin.gov.in or download the CoWIN App
  • Enter a valid mobile number and get the OTP
  • Enter the details required in the page like name, gender, etc. to register..
Step 2: Add or Delete Individuals
  • You can add up to 4 people with the entered phone number
  • Enter the details of the individuals you want to add
  • You can also delete the added members if you want to.
Step 3: Book An Appointment
  • Schedule an appointment at the nearest vaccination center.
  • You can cancel/reschedule the appointment if you want to.
Step 4: Appointment Confirmation
  • Download the appointment slip
  • You will also receive the appointment information via SMS on the registered mobile number.
Step 5: Get Vaccinated
  • Get vaccinated at the booked vaccination center at the scheduled time.
  • Get the second dose of the vaccine after 28 days.
