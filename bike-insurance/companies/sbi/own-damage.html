    SBI Standalone Own-Damage Bike Insurance Policy

    Driving a bike or a scooter comes with certain risks and this can be a source of anxiety for you and your family members. You can negate some of the risks of the road by purchasing insurance for your two-wheeler. It will give you and your two-wheeler a layer of protection by giving compensation for the cost of damages in the event of a mishap of some sort. Moreover, if you purchase an own-damage cover it will take care of own-damage costs for many possible circumstances including the damage caused due to a road accident, case of vandalism, theft, natural or manmade calamity or fires. If purchased alongside the compulsory third party insurance the plan is sure to provide all-round protection for the insurance holder in times of crisis. Additionally, you can also purchase add-on covers which will further enhance your standard policys coverage.

    Standalone Own-Damage Insurance V/S Third Party Insurance V/S Comprehensive Insurance

    Basis

    SBI Third Party Insurance

    SBI Standalone Own-Damage Insurance

    SBI Comprehensive Insurance

    Coverage

    SBIs third party insurance for two-wheelers will provide the policyholder with compensation for third party liabilities in case of a mishap including the cost of property damage, bodily injuries and death. They offer up to Rs 1Lakh for property damage.

    SBIs standalone own damage insurance for two-wheelers will provide the policyholder with compensation for own damage costs under several different possible circumstances such as cases of theft, road accidents and vandalism to name a few.

    SBIs comprehensive insurance plan for two-wheelers will provide the policyholder with compensation for both own damage costs and third party liabilities in the event of a mishap under a single premium. Insurance holders can also opt for a personal accident cover with it.

    Add-ons

    SBI General Insurance does not offer its policyholders add-on covers with third party insurance.

    SBI General Insurance offers several add-on covers with standalone own-damage covers.

    SBI General Insurance offers several add-on covers with comprehensive insurance plans.

    Cost

    SBI third party two-wheeler insurance covers are the least expensive but offer the most basic and compulsory coverage to the insurance holder.

    SBI standalone own damage two-wheeler insurance covers are more expensive than third party insurance covers as they offer better coverage to the insurance holder.

    SBI comprehensive two-wheeler insurance plans are more expensive than third party or standalone own-damage cover because they take care of both aspects under a single premium.

    What is Covered Under an SBI Standalone Own-Damage Policy?

    Following are the aspects that are covered under an SBI standalone own-damage two-wheeler insurance policy:

    • Accidents that occur on the road and damage the two-wheeler.
    • If the two-wheeler catches on fire or there is an explosion of some sort.
    • If there is a case of total loss or a stolen two-wheeler cannot be traced.
    • Natural or manmade disasters that damage the two-wheeler.

    What is Not Covered Under an SBI Standalone Own-Damage Policy?

    Following are the aspects that are not covered under an SBI standalone own-damage two-wheeler insurance policy:

    • Some form of mechanical or electrical failure that causes the two-wheeler to break down.
    • Illegal driving or misuse of the two-wheeler that leads to an unfortunate event.
    • Depreciation, i.e., the lowering value of the two-wheeler due to natural wear and tear.
    • An unfortunate incident that occurs outside India.

    Benefits of SBI Standalone Own-Damage Policy

    Following are the benefits of purchasing an Oriental Two-Wheeler Insurance Policy:

    • It will take care of your own damage costs in the event of a mishap in many different circumstances.
    • As a reward for safe driving the insurance provider will give you no Claim Bonus for every claim-free year.
    • You can purchase add-on covers that can further strengthen the coverage of your existing insurance policy.
